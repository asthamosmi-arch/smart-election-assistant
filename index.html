Voter Guide

1

Registration

Register to vote using Form 6 on the NVSP portal.

2

Find Your Booth

Locate your polling booth via voters.eci.gov.in or helpline 1950.

3

Carry Valid ID

Bring Voter ID, Aadhaar, Passport, Driving License, or any govt photo ID.

4

Cast Your Vote

Press the button on EVM next to your candidate. VVPAT confirms your vote.
