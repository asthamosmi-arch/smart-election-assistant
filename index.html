📖 Voter Guide

1

Registration

Register to vote using Form 6 on the NVSP portal (voters.eci.gov.in). You can also use the Voter Helpline App or submit offline at your local ERO office.

2

Find Your Booth

Locate your polling booth via voters.eci.gov.in, Voter Helpline 1950, or use the Find My Booth section in this app powered by Google Maps.

3

Carry Valid ID

Bring Voter ID (EPIC), Aadhaar Card, Passport, Driving License, PAN Card, or any government-issued photo ID. ECI accepts 12 types of documents.

4

Cast Your Vote

Press the button on EVM next to your candidate. VVPAT will show a printed slip for 7 seconds confirming your vote. Use NOTA if you wish to reject all candidates.

✅ Eligibility & Rights

Your Voting Rights

  • Right to vote by secret ballot — no one can know how you voted
  • Right to choose NOTA if dissatisfied with all candidates
  • Right to assistance if differently-abled or visually impaired
  • Right to complain to Election Commission of India (1950)
  • Right to verify vote on VVPAT slip (7 seconds)
